Tax discs and car tax

  • Car tax: get a tax disc for your vehicle

    Find out how to get a tax disc for your vehicle - online, by phone or at the Post Office, car tax, motorbike, lorry, bus

  • Apply for a duplicate tax disc

    Use form V20 to apply for a duplicate vehicle tax disc

  • Make a SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification)

    Making a SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification) with the reference on your V5C registration certificate, V11 or V85/1

  • Vehicles exempt from vehicle tax

    Find out which vehicles are exempt from vehicle tax (car tax) and how you may still need to display a tax disc

  • Calculate vehicle tax rates

    Use the online tool to find the tax rate for all vehicle types

  • Exchange a tax disc

    Apply at a DVLA local office or fill in form V70 ‘Application to exchange a vehicle tax disc’ and send it to a DVLA local office with any payment and extra documents

  • Apply for a HGV vehicle licence (tax disc) - form V85

    To apply for an HGV vehicle licence (tax disc) download and fill in form V85 and apply in person at a DVLA local office

  • Apply for a tax disc in advance

    If your tax disc is due to run out while you're abroad, you can apply for a new one up to 2 months in advance.

  • Apply for a tax disc refund (form V14)

    Fill in form V14 and send to DVLA to apply for a vehicle tax disc refund

  • Check if a vehicle is taxed

    Check if a vehicle has an up-to-date tax disc (vehicle tax) - make, registration, report, exempt, SORN

  • Get a tax disc without a V11 reminder

    Get a vehicle tax disc (car tax) if you don't have your V11 reminder - apply online or at the Post Office with your V5C or download form V10 and apply by post or at a DVLA local office

  • Importing vehicles into the UK

    Importing vehicles into the UK - register imported vehicles, Mutual Recognition scheme, type approval, vehicle excise duty and VAT

  • Report an untaxed vehicle

    Anonymously report a vehicle on the public road that hasn't got a tax disc

  • Vehicle tax rate tables

    Tables showing the rates for a tax disc (vehicle tax) for different types of vehicles
